## Q (Repeat in Note Format)
“Liquidity zone kya hote hain?”


---

A (Answer – Simple, Ultra-Clear, Money-Focused)

Liquidity zone wo jagah hoti hai jahan badi quantity me orders ek saath pade hote hain.
Banks ko apne huge buy/sell orders fill karne ke liye in zones ki NEED hoti hai.

Price waha jaakar rukta nahi,
price waha jaakar khaata hai.

Liquidity zone teen jagah mostly milta hai:

1) Highs & Lows ke upar/neeche
Retail yahi stop-loss lagata hai.
Banks waha jaake sab SL hit karte hain → liquidity grab.

2) Consolidation (sideways) areas
Sideways chal raha ho toh dono side buyers & sellers jam ho jaate hain.
Ye banks ka buffet table.

3) Imbalances ke aas-paas (fast candle moves)
Jaha price fast bhaaga tha waha opposite orders pending rehte hain.
Price aake fill karta hai → ye bhi liquidity magnet.

Liquidity zone ka basic truth:
Banks ko volume chahiye.
Retail ke stop-loss = Banks ka entry point.

Agar aap liquidity zone pe trade karna seekh gaye,
aap 70% noise ko skip kar doge.
