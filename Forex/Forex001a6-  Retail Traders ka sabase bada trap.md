## Q (Repeat in Note Format)
“Retail traders ka sabse bada psychological trap batao.”


---

A (Answer – Simple, Sharp, Money-Focused)

Retail traders ka sabse bada psychological trap hai “I WILL CATCH THE MOVE.”
Ye ek invisible addiction hai — dopamine ka toxic version.

Market jab tez bhaagta hai, candle lambi hoti hai, chart chamakta hai…
retail ka dimaag sochta hai:
“Abhi entry lo, warna paisa chhoot jayega.”

Ye disease ka naam hai:
FOMO — Fear of Missing Out.

But sach kya hota hai?

– Jis moment aap entry lete ho,
price wapas khisak jata hai.
– Aapka SL hit.
– Aapka confidence down.
– Aapka account bleed.
– Market direction to sahi pakdi thi…
entry timing galat.

Banks isi psychology ka faayda uthate hain.
Woh pehle candle pump karte hain →
retail jump karta →
phir price reverse →
bank entry →
retail lose.

Forex ka real game:
Aapka dimaag price se tez sochna chahiye.
Price kabhi bhaag nahi raha hota.
Sirf manipulation chal rahi hoti hai.

Agar aap FOMO ko kill kar diya,
aap already 50% trader se upar ho.
