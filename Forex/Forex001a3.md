## **A (Answer – Simple, Deep, Money-Focused)**

Banks market ko **predict** nahi karte. Banks market ko **design** karte hain.  
Retail trader sirf unke current ke against tairta hua chhota fish hota hai.

Yeh pura mechanism teen steps me chalta hai:

**1) Liquidity Hunt**  
Banks ko bada position lena hota hai → unko buyers/sellers ka _pool_ chahiye.  
Retail log stop-loss kaha lagate hain?  
– recent highs  
– recent lows  
– tight stop  
Banks price ko waha push karke sabko “clean” karte hain.  
Ye hi stop-loss hunting hai.

**2) Fake Move → Real Move**  
Market pehle **ulta chalke** sabko trap karta hai.  
Example:  
Uptrend start karne se pehle price fall karega.  
Downtrend start se pehle price pump karega.  
Ye trap institutions banate hain taaki retail ulta position me atak jaye.

**3) Discount / Premium Zones (Institutional Pricing)**  
Banks kabhi bhi “random price” pe trade nahi karte.  
Unko discount chahiye (cheap buy) ya premium (expensive sell).  
Price un zones me aane tak kuch bhi kar sakta hai.  
Retail ise randomness samajhta hai, par ye pre-planned hota hai.

Banks ka goal simple hai:  
**Sasta kharidna + Mehenga bechna = Guaranteed edge.**  
Retail ka goal ulta hota hai → emotional entries.

Isliye banks hamesha jeette hain.
