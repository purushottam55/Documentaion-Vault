## Q (Repeat in Note Format)
“Aisi black chije forex ke bare me batao jo mujhe pata na ho.”


---

A (Answer – Dark Truths, Simple, Money-Focused)

Forex ki duniya me kuch andar ki baatein hoti hain jo public ko nahi batayi jaati.
Yahi baatein samajh lo = aapka mindset pro-level ban jata hai.

1) Brokers aapke against trade karte hain.
Most retail brokers market maker hote hain.
Aap loss me jao = unka profit.
Isliye spikes, slippage, stop-loss hit — sab “coincidence” nahi.

2) Market big players ka hunting ground hai.
Banks liquidity hunt karte hain.
Retail (jaise hum) jahan stop-loss lagate hain, price pehle waha jaake sabko “wipe-out” karta hai.
Phir original direction me move karta hai.
Ye trap design hota hai.

3) High win-rate traders zyada jaldi barbaad hote hain.
90% win rate wale mostly ek hi bad trade me khatam ho jaate hain.
Kyun? Risk control ZERO.
Professional traders 40–50% win rate me bhi paisa bana lete hain.

4) News spike 80% manipulation hoti hai, real information nahi.
News candles me maximum liquidation hota hai.
Retail stop-loss clean.
Institutions entry lete hain.
Retail fear me sell/kharid karta rehta hai.

5) Indicator traders ko intentionally confuse karte hain.
Bohot saare indicators broker aur trading platforms ka design hota hai taaki aap overtrade karo.
Market indicator se nahi, liquidity + structure se chalta hai.

6) Forex ka sabse bada scam: “fast money.”
Market slow money deta hai.
Fast sirf losses hote hain.
Jo long game khelta hai, wahi rich hota hai.
